DPRESS – Death Penalty Review: Execution Statistics from the States

Welcome to Death Penalty Review: Execution Statistics from the States (DPRESS)! Before you go any further: By using this site you agree to comply with ICPSR guidelines on the viewing and use of this data. You agree to use these data sets solely for statistical analysis and reporting of aggregated information, and not for investigation of specific individuals or organizations. It also means you agree to not use this data in an attempt to track down any specific individuals or organizations. All names of executed individuals have been removed for this reason, please respect that privacy.

You can go to the Interactive Statistics page for your search options. Our site allows you to search an extensive dataset of executions in what is now the United States from 1608 to 2002 by county, state, crime, year, race, manner of execution, sex, and age at execution.
